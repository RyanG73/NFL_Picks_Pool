# 2026 NFL Picks Pool — Official Rules

*Last updated: May 2026 · Contact: Ryan Gerda (Venmo @Ryan-Gerda)*

---

## 1. Buy-in & Prize Pool

- **Entry fee:** $50 per player, paid via Venmo to @Ryan-Gerda before or shortly after Week 1 kickoff.
- **Prize pool:** 100% of collected buy-ins is paid out — no rake.
- **Prize distribution:** The top **~15% of paid players** share the pot with an escalating prize ladder (higher finishers earn proportionally more). Prizes are calculated automatically when all buy-ins are collected and shown on the standings page.
- **Prizes are paid via Venmo** after the Super Bowl (Week 22) settles, typically on Tuesday.

*Example for 50 paid players ($2,500 pot, 8 prize positions):*
> 1st $550 · 2nd $475 · 3rd $425 · 4th $350 · 5th $275 · 6th $200 · 7th $150 · 8th $75

## 2. Starting Points

- Every player begins with **25,000 points**.
- Points are not real money — they are a scoring currency for the pool. The player with the most points at season's end wins.

---

## 3. Making Picks

- Each week you may wager on **up to 3 NFL games** against the spread (ATS).
- All bets must be in **500-point increments**, with a **minimum of 500 points** per game.
- You may not bet more points than you currently have.
- Picks are submitted via your personal picks link (emailed to you before Week 1 and bookmarkable for the season).

---

## 4. Lock Timing

Picks lock at different times depending on the day:

| Game day | Lock time |
|---|---|
| **Thursday Night Football** | At TNF kickoff (~8:15pm ET Thursday) |
| **Sunday games** | **Saturday at noon ET** |
| **Monday Night Football** | **Saturday at noon ET** |
| Any other day (e.g., Friday) | Saturday at noon ET, or kickoff if earlier |

**Why Saturday noon?** Locking before Sunday's slate prevents last-second injury news from unfairly influencing picks. TNF picks lock at kickoff because they occur before the Saturday deadline.

You may edit picks freely until the lock time. After lock, picks are final for that game.

---

## 5. Picks Reveal

Individual picks become publicly visible on the standings site **at Saturday noon ET** each week (simultaneously with the lock). Before that time, only you and the admin can see your picks.

---

## 6. How Picks Are Scored (ATS)

The spread is set Wednesday morning and **frozen at publication** — it does not move regardless of line changes later in the week.

- **Win:** Your team covers the spread → you win your wagered points (+pick_amount)
- **Loss:** Your team fails to cover → you lose your wagered points (−pick_amount)
- **Push:** Final margin exactly equals the spread → no change (0 net)
- **Voided/Postponed game:** All bets on that game are refunded (0 net)

*Example: Chiefs −7.5 vs Raiders. You bet 5,000 on Chiefs. Chiefs win 24–14 = margin of +10, which beats the spread of 7.5 → WIN +5,000 points.*

---

## 7. Settlement

Games are settled **Tuesday morning** after each week's final scores are confirmed. Updated standings appear on the site by Tuesday afternoon.

---

## 8. No-Bet Penalty

If you fail to submit **any picks** in a given week, you receive an escalating penalty:

| Consecutive missed weeks | Penalty |
|---|---|
| 1st miss | −5,000 points |
| 2nd consecutive miss | −10,000 points |
| 3rd consecutive miss | −15,000 points |
| Each additional | −5,000 × consecutive count |

- The streak resets to zero any week you submit at least one pick.
- **Eliminated players (0 points) are exempt** from no-bet penalties.
- The penalty is applied Saturday at noon ET (at the same time as the pick lock).
- The admin may waive a penalty in exceptional circumstances (documented in the system).

---

## 9. Eliminated Players

If your balance reaches **0 points or below**, you are **permanently eliminated**:

- Your row stays on the standings page (sorted last) with an ELIMINATED badge.
- You may no longer submit picks.
- You cannot re-enter or buy back in to the current season.
- You are not eligible for any prize.

---

## 10. Canceled / Postponed Games

If a game is officially canceled or postponed (NFL POST/CANC status):

- All bets on that game are **voided and refunded** (net 0).
- The admin will notify players via the in-app banner and/or email.
- If a game is voided after settlement has already run, the settlement is reversed.

---

## 11. Ties

If two or more players finish a season with **equal points**:

- Their prize positions are **split evenly**.
- *Example: 2nd and 3rd place tied → each receives (Prize_2nd + Prize_3rd) / 2.*
